Harmonizing 1985-2005 occupation classifications used in Japan's Population Census

Alan Engel
Last update: 23 November 2021

The occupational classifications used in the 1985, 1990, 1995, 2000 and 2005 population censuses slightly differ. In order to compare gender segregation by occupation over this time span, Uchikoshi and Mugiyama (2020) report harmonizing classifications for the 1980 though 2005 population censuses.

    Desired features of coding

  • No leading zeros so that sorting as numbers and sorting as text produce the same results
  • Maintain same ordering as 2005 population census.
  • Enable "group by" aggregation of detailed occupations to produce mid-level and top level occupation class totals.
  • Codes of "not otherwise classified" categories end in "99".
